Micro Enterprize:
A micro enterprise is an enterprise where investment in plant and machinery does not exceed Rs. 25 lakh
e.g.
Investment in plant and machinery = Machine cost + others = 7L+.5L=7,50,000/- So it is Micro Enterprize i.e. Sukshma Udyog

Small Enterprize:
A small enterprise is an enterprise where the investment in plant and machinery is more than Rs. 25 lakh but does not exceed Rs. 5 crore; 
e.g.
If I install 4 such machines then it would be called as Small Enterprize, Laghu Udyog

Medium Enterprize:
A medium enterprise is an enterprise where the investment in plant and machinery is more than Rs.5 crore but does not exceed Rs.10 crore.

Turnover= total sales in a quarter/year
e.g. Sale of 6L cups in a month = 6,00,000 * 0.25 =1,50,000.00 * 3(quarterly)=4,50,000/- * 4(Yearly)=18,00,000/-
SO turnover=18,00,000/- per year.
Prfit per year= Sales - Cost of production = 18,00,000 - 14,40,000 = 3,60,000/-

Profit = Sales - Expenses
====================================

Revenue: Total Sales or Total income 
calcualted quarterly

Profits:-

Gross Profit = Total Sales - Cost of product (Without expenses)

Net Profit = Total Sales - ( Cost of Product + Expenses)

Gross Profit Margin = (Gross Profit/Total Sales) * 100

Net Profit Margin = (Net Profit/Total Sales) * 100

e.g.

Company Revenue: 1,00,000/-
Cost of products: 70,000/-
Gross Profit= 1,00,000 - 70,000 = 30,000/-
Gross Profit Ratio= 30,000 / 1,00,000 *100 = 30 %
Expenses: 5,000/-

Net Profit=1,00,000 - (70,000 + 5000) = 25,000/-
Net Profit Ratio= 25,000 / 1,00,000 *100 = 25 %

60% of 25,000/- = 25,000*60/100 = 15,000/-
40% of 25,000/- = 25,000*40/100 = 10,000/-
